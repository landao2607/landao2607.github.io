budgetify

Duration

September 2020 - December 2020


Role

iOS developer


Skills

Swift 5, Xcode 11

budgetify is an iOS application that helps manage users' money. It allows users to input transactions including incomes and expenses, then presents a visual analysis of monthly spending with a chart. Users will be able to carefully keep track of their budget every month.

App development is another aspect that I am also interested in. In this project, I focused mainly on importing the chart, reading and saving user's inputs within the app.

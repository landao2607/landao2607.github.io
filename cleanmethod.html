about the work

This project aimed at designing two cleaning products that are part of the same product line, which here was a hand wash brand called CleanMethod. This hand wash was designed to solve two main problems: keep hands clean and the environment clean. Being packed with the safest ingredients to both human and the planet, these bottles will help get rid of 99.9% bacteria but also keep skin moisturized. CleanMethod considered it their mission to protect the environment and spread awareness; therefore, their products emphasized on being vegan and recyclable.
